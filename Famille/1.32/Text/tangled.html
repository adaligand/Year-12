Traduction française

It is often difficult. de maintenir a harmonious relationship entre parents et adolescents, as setting the bar too high peut causer du stress in young people. Le besoin for personal space est crucial for their development, mais il devient souvent the subject d’accrochages fréquents. Moreover, la crise d’identité, although normal, est une étape difficile that complicates the situation. Les adolescents se plaignent parfois about having outdated and narrow-minded parents, ce qui contribue to the lack de compréhension mutuelle and can lead des conflits. Commenting on their friends ou faire des reproches constants can deteriorate the relationship et pousser les jeunes to adopt a rebellious or provocative attitude. Pour éviter l’affrontement des points de vue and maintain dialogue, il est essentiel to find common ground et de traiter les adolescents comme des adultes by listening to and respecting their needs.
